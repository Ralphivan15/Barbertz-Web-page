Hairstyles

(From Short to Long Hairstyles)


Clean Shaven Head

Head shaving is a form of body modification which involves shaving the hair from a person’s head.

Buzz Cut

A buzz cut, or wiffle cut, is a variety of short hairstyles, especially where the length of hair is the same on all parts of the head.

Caecar Hairstyle

The Caesar cut is a hairstyle with short, horizontally straight cut bangs.

Crew Cut and Fade

A crew cut is a type of men’s haircut that features short hair on top with short hair graduated in length on the sides and back.

Side Part Hairstyle

The side part is a timeless men’s hairstyle that involves naturally parting your hair across the head to create an elegant, masculine and confident look.

Comb Over Hairstyle

The comb over, a charming classic men’s haircut, is a stylish and versatile choice that symbolizes elegance, sophistication and traditional style, making it a popular look for contemporary gentlemen.

Faux Hawk Hairstyle

A fauxhawk is a hairstyle that resembles a mohawk, with relatively short sides and a longer hair on top.

Fringe Hairstyle

A fringe haircut, also known as bangs, is a style where the hair is cut and styled to create a fringe of hair that falls over the forehead.

Pompadour Hairstyle

The pompadour hairstyle features voluminous, slicked-backed hair held in place by hairspray or hair gel.

Undercut Hairstyle

The undercut is a hairstyle that leaves length on top but has the back and sides of the head closely cut or buzzed.

Man Bun Hairstyle

The man bun is a modern twist on a classic style, where the hair is pulled back and tied into a bun on the top or back of the head.

Swept Back Hairstyle

Swept-back long hair on men is one of the most effective and minimalist styles available.
